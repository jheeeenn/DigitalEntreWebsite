How to Order

Choose your flavour, send your details through Instagram DM, and wait for confirmation before making payment.

01

Choose your flavour

Pick from Original, Oreo, Biscoff, Matcha, or ask what is available for the next batch.

02

DM us on Instagram

Send your order details to our Instagram DM so everything stays in one clear conversation.

03

Wait for confirmation

We will confirm flavour availability, quantity, pickup or delivery details, and total price.

04

Make payment

After confirmation, complete payment using the accepted method shared in our reply.

05

Arrange pickup or delivery

We will coordinate a suitable pickup time or delivery arrangement based on availability.

Pre-order information

Cream & Crumbs works mainly by pre-order so we can prepare the right quantity and avoid unnecessary waste. Please check batch dates and slot availability through Instagram.

Payment methods

Payment details will be shared only after your order is confirmed. Accepted methods may include online transfer or e-wallet, depending on current availability.

Pickup / delivery

Pickup location is confirmed through Instagram DM. Delivery is available for selected nearby areas with a RM0.50 fee per order, and delivery is free when you order 2 or more cakes in the same order.

Important notes

  • Please refrigerate cheesecakes after receiving them.
  • Best enjoyed within 2–3 days because no preservatives are added.
  • Orders are confirmed only after our reply and payment instructions.
  • Availability may change because each batch is prepared in limited quantities.
