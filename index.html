About Cheesyboi

Cheesyboi is a student-made mini cheesecake brand created for a Digital Entrepreneurship project. We focus on small-batch desserts, simple flavours, and a friendly ordering experience through Instagram.

Why choose us?

  • Clear pre-order steps with confirmation before payment

  • Student-run, small-batch, and honest about availability
